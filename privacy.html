Your Privacy Matters: FamilyTree Connect is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to FamilyTree Connect ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Facebook application and services.

By using FamilyTree Connect, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from Facebook

When you log in with Facebook, we receive the following information:

  • Basic Profile Information: Your name, profile picture, and Facebook User ID
  • Email Address: Your email (if you've made it available on Facebook)
  • Friends List: List of your Facebook friends (only if you grant this permission)
  • Public Profile: Any information you've made publicly available on Facebook

2.2 Information You Provide

  • Family Tree Data: Information about family members you add (names, relationships, birth years)
  • Facebook IDs: Facebook User IDs of family members you connect to your tree
  • Custom Information: Any additional notes or information you add to family member profiles

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (features used, time spent)
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Purpose Description
Provide Services Create and display your family tree, connect with Facebook friends
Personalization Show your profile picture, name, and personalized content
Communication Send you updates, notifications, and respond to inquiries
Improvement Analyze usage to improve features and user experience
Security Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues

4. How We Store Your Data

Important: FamilyTree Connect stores your family tree data locally in your browser using localStorage technology. We do NOT store your family tree data on our servers.

4.1 Local Storage

  • Your family tree data is stored on your device's browser
  • Data persists between sessions until you clear browser data or logout
  • Only you have access to this locally stored data
  • We cannot access or retrieve your locally stored data

4.2 What We Store on Our Servers

  • Basic authentication tokens (temporary, for login sessions)
  • Anonymous usage analytics (no personal identifiers)
  • Error logs (for debugging and improvement)

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 When We May Share Information

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the app (e.g., hosting providers)
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5.3 Facebook Sharing

When you use the "Share to Facebook" feature:

  • You control what gets shared
  • Sharing is always optional and requires your explicit action
  • You can choose your audience (public, friends, private) on Facebook

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see Data Deletion section)
  • Export: Download a copy of your family tree data
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect the app from your Facebook account at any time

6.2 Facebook Permissions

You can manage app permissions through Facebook:

  • Visit: Facebook App Settings
  • Find FamilyTree Connect
  • Edit or remove permissions

7. Data Retention

7.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Active Users: Data retained while you use the service
  • Inactive Accounts: Local data remains until you clear browser storage
  • Server Data: Authentication tokens expire after 60 days of inactivity
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request

8. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted
  • Secure Authentication: OAuth 2.0 via Facebook Login
  • Local Storage: Your family tree data never leaves your device
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel can access server data
  • Regular Updates: We keep our security practices up to date

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

FamilyTree Connect is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using FamilyTree Connect, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws.
